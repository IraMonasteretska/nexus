The network specialists for remote business

Nexus Globalcom was conceived around the need for improved access to cloud applications and solutions in regions where connectivity has previously been unable to support these types of technologies.

Nexus Globalcom has been at the forefront of the SD-WAN revolution since the early 2000’s.

Operating across emerging markets in countries with limited and poor connectivity and remote regions with no existing connectivity at all, we have been implementing multipath VPN and SD-WAN solutions, allowing organizations operating in these areas to modernize their business practices and maintain competitiveness in an ever-evolving technical landscape that their businesses operate within.

Experience operating in remote regions

Having years of experience operating in remote regions of Australia and the Pacific, our team have an in-depth understanding of the trials and tribulations faced by organizations on a day to day basis across a range of industry sectors. Through this experience and challenges of our own whilst operating in remote regions, Nexus has developed a range of technical solutions to fill requirements, solve problems faced by our customers and assist in building business practices around these solutions and realizing business efficiencies the cloud technology and business interconnectivity brings.

Best possible solution with the highest level of ongoing support and up time.

We refer to this as Cloud Enablement - whether it’s a secure, accelerated direct interconnect into Microsoft or Amazon Cloud services, a global resilient WAN solution, or a hybrid cloud/on premise implementation, we guarantee to provide the best possible solution with the highest level of ongoing support and up time.
